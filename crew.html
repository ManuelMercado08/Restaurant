About Us

We have our own local saltwater, farm in the outskirts of Atlanta, bringing you the freshest, safest, tastiest seafood, this side of the Louisiana. Our seafood and all of our ingredients are naturally grown in a controlled environment; no steroids, no GMO, no harmful chemicals or pesticides will ever infiltrate our product. Come be a part of something special and join us for an exclusive dining experience.dishes show culinary inspiration that are both delicious and creative.
